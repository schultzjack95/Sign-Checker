Line one will contain:
    length in cm of each line on the sign. Floating point.
    
Second line will contain:
    number of lines on one side of the sign. Integer.
    

Remaining lines each contain a [String]|[int]|[float].
    [String] is the character on a sign piece.
    [int] is the number of sign pieces of that character.
    [float] is the width in cm of that sign piece.
    Ex/ 
        A|2|2.5
        B|3|2.5
        "|1|4
        :|4|4
        -|2|4
        etc
